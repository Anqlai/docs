# Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to the most common questions about GAT. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to [contact our support team](#contact-support).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is GAT?">
    GAT (Generative AI Tool) is an advanced data integration and analysis platform that uses artificial intelligence to help businesses make sense of their data. It connects to multiple data sources, allows you to ask questions in plain language, and provides AI-powered insights and visualizations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does GAT work?">
    GAT works by:
    1. Connecting to your various data sources (CRMs, ERPs, etc.)
    2. Securely syncing and integrating this data
    3. Using natural language processing to understand your questions
    4. Analyzing relevant data using AI algorithms
    5. Generating insights and visualizations based on the analysis
    6. Presenting the results in an easy-to-understand format
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What data sources can GAT connect to?">
    GAT can connect to over 200 data sources, including popular platforms like:
    - Salesforce
    - HubSpot
    - QuickBooks
    - Google Analytics
    - MySQL and PostgreSQL databases
    - And many more!
    
    If you don't see your specific data source listed, [contact us](#contact-support) to check for compatibility.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data secure with GAT?">
    Yes, data security is our top priority. GAT employs:
    - End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest
    - Role-based access control
    - Regular security audits and penetration testing
    - Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection regulations

    For more details, check our [Security Page](#security-page).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need technical skills to use GAT?">
    No, GAT is designed to be user-friendly for both technical and non-technical users. You can ask questions in plain English, and the interface is intuitive. However, for advanced customizations or API integrations, some technical knowledge may be beneficial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How accurate are GAT's insights?">
    GAT's insights are based on your actual data and use advanced AI algorithms for analysis. While extremely accurate, we always recommend using GAT's insights to inform, not replace, human decision-making. The accuracy improves over time as the system learns from more data and user feedback.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I customize GAT for my specific business needs?">
    Yes, GAT offers several customization options:
    - Custom dashboards
    - Tailored data models
    - API access for integration with other tools
    - Custom AI model training for specific industries or use cases (Enterprise plan)

    Contact our [sales team](#sales-contact) to discuss your specific requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to set up GAT?">
    Basic setup can be completed in minutes. However, the time to full implementation depends on:
    - The number and complexity of your data sources
    - The volume of historical data to be synced
    - Any custom integrations or features required

    Typically, most businesses are up and running with GAT within a few days to a week.
  </Accordion>

    - Comprehensive documentation and user guides
    - Email support for all plans
    - Priority phone support for Business and Enterprise plans
    - Dedicated account manager for Enterprise customers
    - Community forums for peer-to-peer assistance

    Check our [Support Page](#support-page) for more details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does GAT cost?">
    GAT offers flexible pricing plans to suit businesses of all sizes:    
    - Professional: Ideal for small businesses, growing companies with more complex needs
    - Enterprise: Customized solutions for large organizations

    Visit our [Pricing Page](#pricing-page) for detailed information and to get a quote tailored to your needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
