# Getting Started with GAT

Welcome to the world of effortless data integration and AI-powered insights! This guide will walk you through the process of setting up GAT and making your first query.

## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [GAT Sign Up Page](https://www.quantifylabs.com/gat-signup) and create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your First Data Source">
    Once logged in, click on "Add Data Source" and choose from over 200 integrations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Follow the prompts to securely authorize GAT to access your chosen data source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Initial Sync">
    GAT will begin syncing your data. This may take a few minutes depending on the size of your dataset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask Your First Question">
    Once syncing is complete, type a question in natural language into the query box, like "What were our top-selling products last month?"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## System Requirements

GAT is a cloud-based solution, so all you need is:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Internet connection
- Supported data source credentials

## Installation

No installation required! GAT runs in the cloud, ensuring you always have access to the latest features and improvements.

<Card title="Pro Tip" icon="lightbulb" iconType="duotone">
  To get the most out of GAT, consider connecting multiple data sources. This allows GAT to provide more comprehensive insights by analyzing data across your entire business ecosystem.
</Card>
